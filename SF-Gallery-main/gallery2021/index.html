VOLUNTEER SELECTION 2021

The recruitment drive is conducted every year to select volunteers for Speaker’s Forum. Selected volunteers are later sorted into different teams with each of them having a specific role that will facilitate with the functioning of the club.Volunteers were recruited based on their interests in the roles they wanted to play as well as their competencies regarding the same. Speakers’ Forum SVCE looks forward to working with the freshly recruited volunteers in the upcoming days.
