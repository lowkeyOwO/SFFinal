Events Conducted By the Club

Model United Nations (MUN), Public Speaking, Debate, and other events are held by the SVCE Speakers Forum. They also hold events on major days such as World Environment Day, Women's Day, Teachers' Day, and so on; these activities give students the opportunity to demonstrate their abilities in a variety of events, developing their communication and interpersonal skills

02

About the club

The Speakers' Forum gives students the tools they need to become better communicators and leaders. By organizing and leading meetings and completing projects, students gain leadership abilities. The projects address and allow individuals to practice skills such as listening, planning, inspiring, and team building. Speakers’ forum mainly focuses on improving speaking talents, offering a platform for sharing various ideas and opinions while learning the intricacies of the language, and empowering one another by expressing their thoughts more articulately. Assisting pupils in organizing various speaking and listening skills in a realistic context.
